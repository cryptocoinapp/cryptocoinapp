CryptoCoin

CryptoCoin - a simple way to stay on top of Bitcoin, the most popular cryptocurrency. Access to live* Bitcoin (BTC) exchange rates from top markets in multiple fiat currencies. Plus, this app provides trending news articles related to Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies such as Litecoin and Dogecoin.

Connect to the following exchange markets: -Coinbase -Blockchain -BTC China

Exchange rates for the following fiat currencies: -USD -EUR -CNY

*live exchange rates auto-update every 30 seconds.
